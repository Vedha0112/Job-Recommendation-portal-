Job Recommendation Portal:

The Job Recommendation Portal is a web-based platform designed to streamline the job search and recruitment process.
It connects job seekers and employers by offering an intuitive interface for job searching, application management, and candidate shortlisting.
The portal provides personalized job recommendations based on user preferences, job categories, and search history, ensuring better job-candidate matching.
Key features include advanced job search with filters, resume management, employer dashboards, application tracking, interview scheduling, real-time notifications, and secure authentication.
Employers can efficiently post jobs, review applications, and manage hiring, while job seekers can search, apply, and track their applications seamlessly.
With a focus on user-friendly navigation, efficient job matching, and a secure hiring process, this portal aims to improve job discovery and recruitment, reducing hiring time and enhancing candidate-employer interactions.
